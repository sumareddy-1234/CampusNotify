ABOUT US

About CampusNotify

CampusNotify is your personalized campus dashboard that keeps you updated with all the essential happenings—from internships and hackathons to club activities and fests. We simplify campus communication by bringing everything into one platform for both students and organizers.

It eliminates the chaos of WhatsApp groups and scattered info by delivering timely, organized updates that matter to you.

Our Mission

We strive to empower students by ensuring no opportunity goes unnoticed. Our mission is to bridge the communication gap between event organizers and students through a tech-savvy, minimalistic, and real-time solution. We want to build a culture where students are involved, informed, and inspired to participate in both academic and extracurricular growth.

Why CampusNotify?

CampusNotify simplifies the way students connect with opportunities. Whether you're looking for internships, hackathons, sports events, or club activities, our platform ensures you're always informed and never left out.

  • 🔔 Real-time alerts for new opportunities across all categories
  • 🎯 Smart filtering options by academic year, department, and event type

  • 📱 Fully responsive design that works smoothly on all devices

How It Works

CampusNotify operates on a simple and efficient two-user system designed to eliminate confusion and improve engagement across campus:

  1. Admins log in through their secure dashboard to post opportunities like internships, hackathons, events, and club announcements. They can categorize posts and add deadlines to automate visibility.
  2. Students can view these opportunities in real time, filter them based on interests, year, or branch, and register for them with a single click. They also receive notifications when deadlines are near or new posts are added.

This streamlined system ensures both sides of the communication process stay connected, updated, and productive.
